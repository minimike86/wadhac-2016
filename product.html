S.C.R.U.T.I.N.Y. Engine®

The S.C.R.U.T.I.N.Y. Engine® is our flagship proprietary risk reporting and 'big-data' network analytics product. It is a revolutionary product with many great exclusive patented features that has made us the envy of our competitors as a result of our technology placing us light-years ahead of the competiton.

S.C.R.U.T.I.N.Y. Engine® enables agility within your business by providing authorised members of your staff with online access to your highly-confidential risk assessment data. Once authenticated we present your data via a clean and concise live dashboard where through our expert analysis you can view your organizations information security strengths and weaknesses.

S.C.R.U.T.I.N.Y. Engine® works by automating the collection of threat intelligence (likelihood), business impact for the automatic calculation of Inherent Risk.
